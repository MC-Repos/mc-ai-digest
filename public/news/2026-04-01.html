JoyStreamer-Flash addresses computational bottlenecks in audio-driven avatar generation through autoregressive diffusion with two key innovations: Progressive Step Bootstrapping (allocating more denoising steps early to prevent error accumulation) and Motion Condition Injection (using noise-corrupted previous frames to maintain temporal coherence). This approach enables real-time inference and theoretically unbounded video synthesis, overcoming the quality degradation and computational overhead limitations of existing DiT-based methods.

This represents a meaningful advancement over prior continual transformer work, which was limited to shallow models with restricted generalization capacity.

Hellinger Multimodal Variational Autoencoders

💼 18 ⚡ 78

This paper proposes HELVAE, a multimodal VAE that replaces standard product/mixture-of-experts aggregation with Hellinger divergence-based opinion pooling, enabling symmetric and efficient multimodal posterior inference without sub-sampling. The approach leverages moment-matching approximations derived from Hölder pooling (α=0.5) to learn more expressive latent representations that improve with additional modalities while achieving better generative-inference trade-offs. This represents a theoretically grounded alternative to existing aggregation schemes with practical efficiency gains for weakly-supervised multimodal learning scenarios.

KARMA: Knowledge-Action Regularized Multimodal Alignment for Personalized Search at Taobao

cs.AI updates on arXiv.org · 2026-04-01

💼 18 ⚡ 35

KARMA addresses the "Knowledge-Action Gap" in applying LLMs to personalized search by using semantic reconstruction as a training regularizer, preventing semantic collapse (attention sinks) that occurs when fine-tuning on discriminative objectives alone. The framework enables LLMs to simultaneously preserve pre-trained semantic knowledge while aligning with task-specific personalization objectives, demonstrating improved generalization on Taobao's next-item prediction task. This approach reveals a critical training stability challenge in deploying large language models to industrial recommendation systems and offers a practical solution through multi-objective regularization.
