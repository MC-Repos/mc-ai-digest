First Logit Boosting: Visual Grounding Method to Mitigate Object Hallucination in Large Vision-Language Models

cs.AI updates on arXiv.org · 2026-04-02

💼 23 ⚡ 72

This paper introduces First Logit Boosting (FLB), a training-free method that mitigates object hallucination in Large Vision-Language Models by storing and reinforcing the initial token's logit distribution throughout generation, addressing the "long-term decay" problem where visual grounding deteriorates as language priors dominate later tokens. FLB offers a practical advantage over existing approaches by requiring no retraining, external models, or complex architectural changes while improving upon prior training-free methods like Contrastive Decoding. The technique targets a fundamental weakness in LVLMs—confabulating non-existent objects—which is critical for reliable deployment in real-world multimodal applications.
