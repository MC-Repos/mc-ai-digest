cs.CV updates on arXiv.org · 2026-01-15

💼 28 ⚡ 78

JoyAvatar-Flash introduces an autoregressive diffusion approach for real-time audio-driven avatar generation that overcomes computational constraints and enables infinite-length video synthesis through three key innovations: Progressive Step Bootstrapping prioritizes denoising early frames to minimize error accumulation, Motion Condition Injection maintains temporal coherence using noise-corrupted frame conditioning, and Unbounded RoPE with cache-reset handling extends sequence length without position encoding limitations. This addresses critical bottlenecks in DiT-based methods, making long-form avatar generation practical for deployment while maintaining quality across extended sequences.

PhyRPR: Training-Free Physics-Constrained Video Generation

PhyRPR introduces a training-free three-stage pipeline that decouples physical reasoning from visual synthesis in video generation—using multimodal models for physics understanding, deterministic motion planning for coarse scaffold generation, and latent space fusion during diffusion sampling to enforce physical constraints. This approach addresses a critical limitation in current diffusion-based video models that conflate high-level physics with low-level visual details, enabling physically plausible synthesis without model retraining. The method's practical significance lies in enabling physics-aware video generation through post-hoc constraint injection rather than requiring physics-specific training objectives.

Video-MSR: Benchmarking Multi-hop Spatial Reasoning Capabilities of MLLMs

cs.CV updates on arXiv.org · 2026-01-15

💼 15 ⚡ 72

Video-MSR introduces the first benchmark for evaluating multi-hop spatial reasoning in MLLMs across dynamic video scenarios, addressing a critical gap where existing benchmarks only assess single-step perception tasks. The benchmark features 3,052 curated video instances with 4,993 QA pairs spanning four reasoning tasks: constrained localization, chain-based reference retrieval, route planning, and counterfactual physical deduction. This work establishes rigorous evaluation methodology for complex visual-spatial reasoning chains, enabling systematic assessment of MLLM capabilities beyond static image understanding.
